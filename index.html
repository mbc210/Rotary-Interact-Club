About Us

What is Interact?

Interact is Rotary International's service club for young people. The name joins two words — International and Action — and that is exactly what we do. Sponsored by our local Rotary Club, the Interact Club of Chinatown brings students together to take on projects that make a real difference.

Every year our members complete two kinds of projects: one that helps our school and Chinatown community, and one that promotes international understanding. Along the way we grow as leaders, make lasting friendships, and discover the joy of Service Above Self.

  • Open to students ages 12–18
  • Guided and supported by sponsoring Rotarians
  • Member-led projects, real responsibility
  • Part of a global network of 200,000+ Interactors

Our Mission

To serve the people of Chinatown — our elders, families, students, and small businesses — while connecting our neighborhood to a worldwide movement of young people committed to peace, goodwill, and service.

“Service Above Self.” — The Rotary motto we live by

What We Do

Four avenues of service

Our work follows Rotary's avenues of service. Each one is a different way for members to lead, contribute, and grow.

🤝

Community Service

Neighborhood clean-ups, senior outreach, food drives, and tutoring for younger students — practical help where Chinatown needs it most.

🌏

International Service

Fundraisers and awareness campaigns that support global causes and connect us with Interact clubs in other countries.

🏮

Cultural Celebration

Lunar New Year events, Mid-Autumn gatherings, and heritage projects that keep Chinatown's traditions vibrant for the next generation.

🌱

Leadership Development

Workshops, club officer roles, and Rotary youth programs that help members build confidence, public speaking, and teamwork.

Our Work

Projects in motion

A look at the initiatives our members are leading this year.

🏮
Community

Lunar New Year Street Clean-Up

Members partner with local businesses to brighten Chinatown's main streets before the new year celebrations, welcoming visitors with a clean, festive neighborhood.

👵
Outreach

Elder Friendship Visits

Weekly visits and grocery help for Chinatown seniors — combating isolation with conversation, technology lessons, and a friendly hand.

📚
Education

After-School Tutoring

Our students mentor younger children in reading and math, with bilingual support for families new to the language.

🥡
Hunger

Community Food Drive

Seasonal collections that stock local pantries and deliver warm meals to families and unhoused neighbors across the district.

🌏
International

Clean Water Fundraiser

A year-long campaign supporting Rotary's global clean-water projects, run through bake sales, festivals, and a charity walk.

🎨
Culture

Heritage Mural Project

Working with local artists, members help design a community mural celebrating Chinatown's history and the families who built it.

Get Involved

Join the club

If you are a student between 12 and 18 with a heart for service, there is a place for you here. No experience needed — just bring your energy and ideas. Parents, teachers, and Rotarians who want to support our work are always welcome too.

  • Students: attend a meeting and sign up — it's free to join.
  • Families: volunteer at an event or help us plan.
  • Sponsors: support a project or a club scholarship.

Meetings are held on the second Tuesday of each month at the Chinatown Community Center. We would love to meet you.
