Club life

Recent highlights

A few of the projects our members showed up for this year.

May 2026

Repainting Doyers Street

We joined the volunteer crew rolling out "Yangtze," the 2026 Doyers Street ground mural by SMOE Studio — blues, reds, and golds turning the street into a flowing river before its May 15 unveiling.

Doyers Street Plaza
Rotary Day of Service

The Red Run

Our crew took over the East River Esplanade for Rotary Day of Service — laps under the Manhattan Bridge, water-station duty, and a group photo we're proud of.

East River Esplanade
October

Halloween on the block

Candy jars, balloon animals, and a very busy table — we handed out treats to neighborhood trick-or-treaters at the Chinatown Halloween block party.

Chinatown
Year-round

Cards for our elders

Members hand-make holiday and birthday cards for homebound seniors in the neighborhood — small notes that mean a lot, delivered with our partner organizations.
